About Lilly

Eli Lilly and Company is a global pharmaceutical company headquartered in Indianapolis, Indiana, along with other offices in 18 countries. Its products are sold in approximately 125 countries. The company was founded in 1876 by Col. Eli Lilly, a pharmaceutical chemist and veteran of the American Civil War, after whom the company was named.

Lilly's notable achievements include being the first company to mass-produce the polio vaccine. Lilly has never merged with others and has always stayed Lilly. Eli Lilly is a company that wishes to make an impact in the 21st century by making medicine help people live longer healthier lives.

“Take what you find here and make it better and better.”

Colonel Eli Lilly
