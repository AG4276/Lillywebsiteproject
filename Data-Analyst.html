Database Analyst is a person doing what they do, analyzing data.

She would draw a model or have a picture of what the data looks like. Most of the time it is a conceptional model but sometimes it can turn into a physical model. She checks if tools and supplies bought by the company are industry compatible.

She works together with other companies such as financial trail to figure out the best possible outcome from the data. She spends most of their time mapping out the data, taking other data and putting it in their database, and merging external source data into the database. She would notify or alert if there is a problem in the data. Tool mostly used would be Excel Spreadsheets.
