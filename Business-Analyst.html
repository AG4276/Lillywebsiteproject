The Executive Lead or Business Analyst is the person in between the technical side of a product and the business side.

Nora figures out the requirements of the product and its production such as the time and cost. She figures out a timeline and who is involved in the timeline as well as a design for the product. She talks to the business about what they want and also to the developers about what the product can actually do.

She accesses the database and pulls any data that is needed. Weekly meetings are set up to check what has been done and get status reports for the businesses. She write a lot documents and completes paper such as validation documentation. Tools used would be Outlook for email and calendar, Excel, Powerpoint, Microsoft Word and Skype.
